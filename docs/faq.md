# FAQ

  

# Who is behind MyFavoriteAlbums?

  

MyFavoriteAlbums was written and coded by Professor Chris Holtrom, a University of Washington professor specializing in technical and professional writing.

  

# Can I add my own data onto MyFavoriteAlbums?

  

MyFavoriteAlbums does not have the ability to provide your own data through the Graphical User Interface. However, you can provide your own data via a CSV file.

  

# What is the breakdown of how an album is ranked/rated?

  

The developer gave his own personal ratings and rankings, meaning the score was based on personal enjoyment rather than a specific set of qualifications to meet a number score.

  

# Is this a music recommendation webpage?

  

No. MyFavoriteAlbums is not meant to recommend albums but rather visualize and organize the developer’s personal taste; however, many songs, bands, and artists can be discovered by engaging with the interface.

  

# Do I need an account to access MyFavoriteAlbums?

  

No. An account is not needed, the link provides all the access needed: [https://cholstro.shinyapps.io/shiny-music](https://cholstro.shinyapps.io/shiny-music)

  

# Is the dataset updated automatically?

  

No. The developer must update the dataset and republish the app.

  

# Are cookies used?

Only standard browser session behavior applies.
