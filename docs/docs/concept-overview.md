Conceptual Overview for General users

What is MyFavoriteAlbums?

MyFavoriteAlbums is an interactive data analysis software that showcases a curated collection of albums from the developer’s personal favorites, ranging from 1993 to 2024. This software allows users to explore albums and artists across this range of years utilizing rankings, ratings, comparative graphs, and filters.

This software is designed to share and visualize a personal music collection dataset. Instead of simply listing albums, this software gives the opportunity to analyze trends, compare artists, and visualize an opinionated music taste in a fun and interactive way.

Key Concepts and Definitions

Album Rating

A numerical score ranging from 0-10 that reflects the developers personal evaluation on a specific album, scaling from 0, illustrating dislike, all the way to 10, illustrating a high score of enjoyment.

Average Artist Rating

The mean of all ratings for albums by a particular artist

Album Ranking

A position in a numerical list where albums by year are preferred over the other where 1 is the highest.

Comparative Graph

A graphical representation of data comparing artists and their albums released from 1993 to 2024 and the rating of the album in the given year

Filter

A checked box that excludes information, such as excluding artist EP’s

Drop Down Box

An element that displays a hidden list of options when clicked, allowing users to choose between options. In the case of MyFavoriteAlbums, different artists, bands, or albums.

What the Software is capable of

The software allows users to:

View the number one albums in the range of years
Filter a specific year to view the ranking of albums in the given year
Filter band or artist to view all their albums in the range of years and view each individual ranking
Filter by number of albums released excluding EP’s and live albums
Compare two artists or bands visualizing on a graph the trajectory of rating across years
Filter by top albums not owned on vinyl
What the Software is incapable of

The software does not allow users to:

Stream selected music
Input any comments or opinions
See albums and years not implemented by the developer
