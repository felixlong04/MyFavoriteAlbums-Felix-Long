# Troubleshooting

  

**What happens if I get disconnected from the server?**
![Alt text](userref1.png)
After a certain amount of time not using MyFavoriteAlbums, you may be disconnected from the server. When this happens, you can click the reload button or refresh your browser. Once you reload or refresh, you will be reconnected; however, any changes you made to filters will be lost.
